Digit Converter Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2023

Thank you for choosing Digit Converter. Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information from you when you use our mobile application (the “Digit Converter”).

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you when you use Digit Converter. The App does not require any user registration or account creation.

The only information we collect is anonymous analytics data to help us understand usage patterns and improve the App. This may include information like the features used, session length, devices and operating systems used, and approximate location at the city level. No personal data is collected.

Use of Information

The analytics information we collect is used to improve the App, prioritize new features, and debug issues. We may use aggregate statistics about usage patterns in marketing materials and to demonstrate popularity of the App. The data is anonymized and no personal information is identifiable.

Information Disclosure

We do not disclose any information collected from the App to third parties. The limited analytics data is maintained internally and no personal identifiers are attached.

If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with a legal requirement like a subpoena, court order, or search warrant. We will make reasonable efforts to notify users before complying unless we are legally restricted.

Data Retention

We only retain anonymous analytics data for as long as is useful to maintain and improve the App, typically 12 months. Information is then disposed securely. We do not retain personal data since none is collected.

Children's Privacy

The App is not targeted to or intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided information without consent, we will delete it as soon as possible.

Changes to Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our App. We encourage you to review the policy periodically for any updates. Changes become effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy in the App.
